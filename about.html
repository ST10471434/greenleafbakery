Our Story

From a home kitchen to your table

The GreenLeaf Journey

GreenLeaf Bakery was founded in 2020 by Emma Martinez, a pastry chef with a passion for organic ingredients and sustainable practices. What began as a small home-based micro-bakery during the pandemic quickly grew into a beloved community staple. Emma's sourdough starters, made from heirloom grains, gained a loyal following among neighbours craving authentic, wholesome bread.

By 2022, demand had outgrown Emma's home kitchen. With support from the local community and a small business grant, GreenLeaf Bakery opened its first retail location. Today, we operate two storefronts and supply our organic baked goods to over 25 cafes and restaurants across the region.

We believe that good food should be good for the planet too. That's why every decision - from ingredient sourcing to packaging - is made with environmental responsibility in mind.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, wholesome baked goods made from 100% organic, locally-sourced ingredients while minimising our environmental impact.

Our Vision

To become the leading sustainable bakery in the region, inspiring other food businesses to adopt eco-friendly practices.

Our Core Values

🌱 Sustainability

Zero-waste baking, compostable packaging, and energy-efficient equipment.

🤝 Community

✨ Quality

❤️ Transparency
